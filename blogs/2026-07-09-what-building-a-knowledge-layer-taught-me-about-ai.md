Key Takeaways Connecting AI agents to APIs isn’t enough. They need a normalized knowledge layer to reason over verified data instead of inventing answers. Structured evidence stored in an intermediate layer prevents hallucinations by creating a deterministic boundary between retrieval and reasoning.

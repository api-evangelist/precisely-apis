<h2>Why Clean Core Matters to SAP Teams</h2>
<p>SAP’s Clean Core approach is centered on reducing unnecessary customizations inside the ERP core so organizations can simplify upgrades, improve system maintainability, and innovate faster.</p>
<p>If you’re part of an SAP team, you know that this is often easier said than done.</p>
<p>Even in highly strategic transformation programs, day-to-day execution still depends on reliable data movement and process execution. Businesses need to update material data, pricing records, finance data, and other SAP objects at scale. Historically, many of these activities have relied on custom code, manual workarounds, or legacy approaches that increase complexity over time.</p>
<p>As a result, organizations often face a difficult balancing act: how to modernize the ERP landscape while preserving the efficiency and continuity of the processes the business depends on every day.</p>
